Launch the Crypto Analyst Team terminal chat interface.

Five AI analysts (ARIA, MARCUS, NOVA, REX, ZEN) respond to your crypto questions with live market data from CoinGecko and Binance.

**Usage after launch:**
- Type any question mentioning a coin (BTC, ETH, SOL, etc.) to get all 5 analysts responding with live data
- Address one analyst: `ARIA, what does the RSI say about ETH?`
- `/analyze BTC` — full team breakdown with fresh data fetch
- `/performance` — analyst track records and open position P&L
- `/lookback BTC 30` — AI post-mortem on past 30 days of BTC calls (saves as memory)
- `/history` — last 30 days of saved recommendations
- `/team` — analyst bios
- `/help` — all commands

cd crypto_analyst_team && python main.py
